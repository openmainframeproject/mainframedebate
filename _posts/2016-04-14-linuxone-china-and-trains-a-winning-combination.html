As the high speed train that is LinuxONE continues to roll (sorry it had to be done), great to see another client reference coming out today from a Chinese client, the catchily titled Nanchong Dandai Transport Industry Group.  From what we can glean from the press release it appears that the LinuxONE system will provide the foundation for a new mobile app that will service travelers in 64 towns and cities and provide them with a compelling way to purchase tickets, whilst also providing Dandai with some very valuable client insight.

Expect to see more of these types of wins from IBM as clients increasingly see Linux as mission critical and therefore demand; higher availability, performance, scale and security from the underlying platform that supports their deployment.

So many possibilities...

The recent announcement of the IBM z13 is a reflection on how businesses and our lives have changed in the last 10 years. The way we live, the way we act, the way we interact; all this has changed. Smart phone technologies can b regarded as the core reason behind this. Since the advent of the smart phone our behavior has changed. Our general day-to-day behavior has changed; this has led to new expectations, which in turn create a new demand for information to be consumed.
